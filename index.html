Cloud Based Home Safety System (CBHSS)

Stay Safe, Stay Connected: Your Cloud Home Protector.

About Us

Cloud-Based Home Safety System aims to provide a cost-effective solution to enhance home safety, particularly when homes are unoccupied.To provide comprehensive protection for houses in Malaysia, this project introduces a multi-faceted approach.

The objectives of Cloud-Based Home Safety System are as below:

  • To develop a prototype of a home safety system at an affordable cost.
  • To implement gas and fire safety by offering auto extinguishing systems that can prevent potential disasters.
  • To alert users with a photo through mobile phone when a motion is detected.

Services

This project is divided into three major modules: motion alert, fire detection and extinguishment, as well as gas and smoke detector with absorber fans.

Motion Alert System

When any motion is detected, it sends instant alerts with photo via platforms like Telegram. This module is primarily designed for burglary prevention.

Fire Detection and Extinguishment

When fire is detected at the sensor, it will attempt to extinguish the flame with a sprinkler before the flame spreads.

Gas Detection

When the sensor detects gas, the fan goes on automatically. When the sensor detects no gas, the fan shuts off immediately

Energy Efficiency

CBHSS saves energy as it uses components and sensors that require minimal energy, resulting in reducing the overall energy consumption

Real-Time Notifications

CBHSS provides real time notifications when it detects any hazards to the registered users

Cost Effective

CBHSS does not require a 24-hour monitoring services, and all the essential security features come under the same roof and do not require to be installed separately from different vendors

IoT (Internet of Things)

The interconnection of physical devices (sensors, actuators) embedded with technology to communicate and exchange data, contributing to the system's functionality.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Does this system requires Internet?

    The system is reliant on continuous Internet or power supply to send real time alerts and notifications to users.

  • What kind of features does this system covers?

    This system covers burglary, fire and gas or smoke detection safety features.

  • Can I access the system from multiple devices?

    Yes, you can access the system from any internet-enabled device, including smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers. Simply log in to the mobile app or web portal using your credentials to view your home's security status and manage system settings.
